Bath & Body Works needed a breakthrough in cost validation for their top 50 raw materials. As Product Manager & Technical Lead, I spearheaded the design and delivery of a GPT-powered sourcing analysis tool that leverages AI to detect anomalies in supplier bids, driving smarter, faster, and more reliable procurement decisions.

## Features

- **AI-driven Anomaly Detection**: Instantly flags outliers and inconsistencies in supplier data
- **Excel Modeling**: Seamless integration for business users

## Tech Stack

- **Python**, **OpenAI API**, **Excel**

## Impact

- Enabled data-driven, AI-powered procurement for a Fortune 500 retailer
- Reduced manual review time and improved cost accuracy
